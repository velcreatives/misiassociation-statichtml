MALAYSIAN INDIAN SPACE INTELLECTUALS ASSOCIATION (MISI)

As how a small seed grows into a big tree, a simple and similar intention among a group of volunteers became a fundamental reason on the formation of this association. Individuals from various fields of expertise and experiences got introduced interacted through many educational and non-educational events organised by NGOs.

The unity and momentum gained throughout the journey as a team of volunteers, encouraged us to move further and focus on social responsibilities especially on younger generations. With the knowledge and experience, we voluntarily developed and implemented many types of educational programs for primary schools and for public since year 2012. The overwhelming support given by the participated parties widen our project scales particularly on astronomy. A proper research and development on the specific project helped us to progress gradually with higher objectives and greater responsibilities.

At these circumstances, we made a decision as a team to form an association to spread our noble intention throughout the nation. On 8th January 2016, our association (MALAYSIAN INDIAN SPACE INTELLECTUALS ASSOCIATION) was officially registered under Register of Society (ROS).
