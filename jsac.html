Junior Science Adventurers’ Camp (JSAC)

Introduction

The purpose of JSAC is to further instil an interest among the students to explore more in the field of science. JSAC designed in a very interesting & unique way that enables the participants to discover the main five sections present in science. Moreover, this camp will boost-up the participants’ critical thinking skills through certain mathematical riddles and problems.

Objective

  • To instil an interest in learning science and maths.
  • To introduce various field of science apart from the text book content.
  • To teach the students the skills and ways of exploring into the world of science.
  • To keep in touch of terms in Science in English.
  • To show the depth of science to students.
